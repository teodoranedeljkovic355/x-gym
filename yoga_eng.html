Prenatal yoga refers to all yoga exercises that are practiced during pregnancy. The word yoga means integrity and unification. Through the practice of yoga we strive to establish mental, physical and spiritual wholeness with ourselves, but also with the whole world around us. Prenatal yoga, in addition to all the above, creates a special connection between the mother and the child who grows up in her, and prepares the woman completely for childbirth and all the challenges that motherhood brings with it.

4/5
4/5
Hata yoga is the most common branch of yoga today. Hatha yoga classes are held all over the world for the benefit of a person's physical and mental condition, and they mostly consist of physical positions or asanas, and in any case the greatest attention is paid to them. Analyzing the meaning of the word itself in its original form in Sanskrit - "Hatha", we come across two roots. The first is "HAM" - which represents solar energy, a vital force that manifests itself as dynamism and extroversion.

Ashtanga yoga


45min
5/5
4/5
Initially, the focus is on daily practice of asanas so that the body and sensory organs become flexible, which is a prerequisite for the stability of the mind. Ashtanga yoga techniques are grouped into six different series, each of which should be skillfully mastered in the correct order before we embark on mastering the next one. The emphasis is on vinyasa, a specific breathing / movement system that generates heat, induces internal purification and improves circulation.
