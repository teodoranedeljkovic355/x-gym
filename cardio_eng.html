HIIT cardio


25min
4/5
5/5
HIIT training is based on the so-called tabata training programming system. It consists of 20 seconds of work and 10 seconds of break that alternate within 8 rounds of different exercises. Intervals are not longer than 30 to 60 seconds in the effort phase, ie. high intensity, followed by intervals of low intensity of 2-3 minutes of relaxation, ie. active rest. HIIT cardio training should last a total of 20 to 25 minutes per workout, 3 times a week.

Zumba cardio


60min
2/5
5/5
ZUMBA is an energetic program that combines elements of Latin dances with fitness movements. Thanks to such a concept, in addition to standard training, you also learn dance movements, while experiencing a unique combination of fun and adrenaline. Numerous effectiveness of this training refers to the following: it improves coordination, body flexibility and endurance, strengthens the cardiovascular system, actively burns fat (with the loss of almost 2000 calories per workout).
