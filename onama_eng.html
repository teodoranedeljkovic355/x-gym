About us



Why to choose us?

X-FITNESS is a specialist in Belgrade for group fitness programs with more than 200 fitness classes per month and 15 different types of programs. From Les Mills program to yoga, pilates, cardio box… Our philosophy is based on humanistic and sports values ​​and our team of professionals is always ready to welcome you, listen to you and provide support. It is a team with which you will have fun and get the desired results. With first-class equipment, efficient fitness programs, a social atmosphere and instructors who are inspired by fitness and oriented to give you a sporty feeling, you will be encouraged to reach the desired physical shape.
