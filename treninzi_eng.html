Core training

Coach: Marko Petrović
19 komentara

Therapeutic Pilates is based on the basic principles of Stott Pilates (see Stott Pilates), but since it is intended for those who have problems in the back and neck as well as in the knee, hip and shoulder joint, it also has its peculiarities. Technique modified for maximum protection and efficiency in patients with spinal diseases. There are basic Pilates principles of connection between breathing and movement, precision, harmony of movements, centering (strengthening the center of the body), stability, range of motion. The essence is in strengthening the musculature of the "center of the body", that is. the abdominal muscles of the back and pelvis, which are responsible for maintaining good posture and the health of the spine.

Pilates

Coach: Marija Pantić
27 komentara

If you want to tighten your muscles and fix the line, and at the same time learn to breathe and hold properly, Pilates is the right thing for you. This type of fitness helps relieve stress and contributes to relaxation. Pilates largely avoids high impact, high power output, and heavy muscular and skeletal loading. You will build your body in a completely different way. Without aggressive and strenuous exercises, Pilates brings you a form that will be a role model for others. You will have a better posture and a general sense of balance in life. Achieve a balance of body and mind.

Yoga

Coach: Una Čolić
17 komentara

Yoga (joga) je drevna disciplina koja se razvija hiljadama godina. U pitanju je opuštajući trening koji će poboljšati tvoju fleksibilnost i eliminisati stres. The goal of Yoga is to leave the gym with a wide smile, after achieving the unity of body, heart and mind. Stretching and relaxation exercises are also part of this training. Yoga is a workout suitable for everyone - it doesn't matter if you choose Yoga to eliminate stress, improve flexibility or improve body strength! Every movement can be adapted to you, so it doesn't matter if you are a beginner, athlete, recreational or just want to eliminate stress.


Cardio training

Coach: Aleksa Perić
23 komentara

Cardio training is the most effective way to remove fat deposits, achieve and maintain fitness. Cardio exercises are necessary for any effective training program because they encourage fat burning, but they are also good for the overall health of the body. Also, if you haven't exercised for a long time and don't have the strength for other workouts, you can always adjust your cardio training to your abilities. Cardio exercises speed up your metabolism, strengthen your heart and lungs, and reduce your resting heart rate. Cardio exercises increase blood flow to the brain, which improves concentration. They also contribute to reducing stress and strengthening the immune system.
